📜 How to Play
Chess Rules apply! Pieces move normally.

Landing on a property:
• Unowned → Buy it or pass
• You own it → Safe
• Opponent owns it → Pay rent!

Capturing an opponent's piece claims their most expensive property too!

Bankruptcy ($0 or less) = You lose!
Checkmate = You lose!

Rent = $20–$200 based on property group.
