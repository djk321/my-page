How to Play

Move Piece — select your piece, then click a highlighted square to move or capture.
Remove Block — pull out any red-highlighted block (no piece on top).
Cascade — if an entire row empties, all blocks above fall, capturing any pieces caught in the collapse.
Win — capture the opponent's Keep (♔ / ♚) to win. One action per turn.

Piece Movement

♔ Keep — 1 step any direction
♖ Rook — slides horizontally or vertically
♗ Bishop — slides diagonally
♘ Knight — L-shape jump
♙ Pawn — 1 step forward; captures diagonally
