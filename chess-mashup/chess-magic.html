Quick rules
• Standard chess movement and capture rules apply throughout
• You play White — the AI controls Black
• Each turn you gain +1 mana (max grows each round, up to 10)
• Click a piece to select it, then click a square to move
• With a piece selected, press Cast to activate its spell — if you have enough mana
• Stunned pieces (faded) skip their turn · Shielded pieces (gold outline) cannot be captured
• Queen's Arcane Surge and Knight's Phantom Leap require clicking a target square after casting
• Win by capturing the enemy King — checkmate is not enforced, so hunt it down!
