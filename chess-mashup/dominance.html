QUICK RULES
• 6×6 board · 21 domino tiles, each showing two chess pieces
• Draw 4 tiles; pool refills your hand each turn
• Place a tile (1×2) anywhere it fits on the board
• On placement, each piece captures adjacent enemies using its chess movement
• Capturing the King wins instantly
• Board fills without King capture → highest score wins
