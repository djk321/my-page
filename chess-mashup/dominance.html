📜 How to Play
Chess rules apply.

18 domino tiles are hidden face-down across the board. Land on one to flip it — the LEFT pip triggers a power!

Doubles (equal pips) also give a $50 bonus!

Properties: Land on unowned → buy. Opponent's → pay rent.

Topple lets you remove any adjacent enemy piece (not King) outside normal chess rules.

Win: Checkmate OR bankrupt opponent.
