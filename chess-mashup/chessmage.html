CHECKMAGE
"Chess decides where your armies move. Magic decides how the battlefield changes."
Standard chess movement — but every piece has HP and Attack. Pieces wound each other instead of instantly dying. Cast spells from your hand to change the course of battle. Win by checkmate OR by reducing the enemy King's 20 Life Points to zero.

Quick rules
• Pieces have HP — a Knight (2 ATK) vs a Rook (3 HP) wounds but doesn't kill it
• Pieces at 0 HP are removed. The King has 20 Life Points instead of standard capture
• Each turn: gain mana, cast spells from hand, move a piece, then resolve combat
• Occupy d4/d5/e4/e5 for +1 bonus mana next turn (gold-tinted squares)
• Win by capturing the King or reducing its LP to 0 — or trap it with no moves
• You play White vs the AI. Queen can cast one spell for free per turn
