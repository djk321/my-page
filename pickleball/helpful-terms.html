📖 Helpful Terms

Pickleball vocabulary you need to know

VolleyHitting the ball before it bounces on your side of the court
DropSoft shot from backcourt that lands in the opponent's kitchen
KitchenNon-volley zone near the net where, if you step in, you can't volley
DriveFast, flat shot hit with power toward opponent
Kitchen LineLine marking the edge of the non-volley zone (the kitchen)
Split-StepSmall hop for balance before reacting to opponent's shot
DinkSoft shot that lands in the opponent's kitchen (non-volley zone)
Chicken WingAn awkward bent-elbow defensive shot, usually forced by a body attack
ATPAround-the-Post — a shot that travels around the net post instead of over it; legal and highly effective when pulled wide
ErneA volley hit while jumping to the side of the post, outside the kitchen; surprise attack that's hard to defend
Speed-UpSuddenly accelerating a dink into a fast drive to catch opponents off guard
ResetA soft, controlled shot used to slow down a fast rally and return to a neutral dinking exchange
StackingA doubles positioning strategy where both players line up on the same side after a serve or return to keep dominant sides in play
Third Shot DropThe serving team's 3rd shot — a soft arc into the kitchen that gives them time to advance to the NVZ
NVZNon-Volley Zone — official name for the kitchen; a 7-foot zone on each side of the net where volleys are illegal
LobA high arcing shot intended to sail over opponents at the net and land deep in their backcourt
